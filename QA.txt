Q:What is a memory that really changed who you are today?

A:I was sitting on a wall close to the lake and contemplated about moving to Berlin. My best friend walked by and said I should listen to my gut what I want to do. That was the moment I decided to move.

Q:What feelings do you remember?

A:I remember being confused and unsure what might happen when I take the decision. But also supported in my decision. It was also cold.

Q:Do you remember any specific sounds or music from this time?

A:Mostly the sound of the water

Q:How did this memory change the way you think or act?

A:It opened a lot of opportunities.

Q:When you think about it what do you see?

A:I See the dark night and my best friend walking towards me. There is also the water and some trees on the promenade.
